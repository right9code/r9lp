# Descriptive Paragraph (Object)

## 📌 What is a Descriptive Paragraph about an Object?
A descriptive paragraph about an **object** explains what an item looks like, its purpose, its parts, and what makes it special or useful. The goal is to provide enough detail so that the reader can clearly visualize the object.

## 📝 Format / Structure (Word Limit: 100-120 words)

1. **Heading/Title**: Name of the object.
2. **Introduction (1-2 sentences)**: 
   - Identify the object.
   - State to whom it belongs or how you got it.
3. **Body (Physical Details & Function) (5-6 sentences)**:
   - **Physical appearance**: Shape, size, color, material, texture, weight.
   - **Components**: Mention any special parts or features.
   - **Function**: What is it used for? How does it work?
4. **Conclusion (1-2 sentences)**:
   - Your personal connection or opinion about the object.
   - Why it is valuable or important to you.

## ⚖️ CBSE Marking Scheme (Typically 5 Marks)
- **Format (Title)**: 1 Mark
- **Content**: 2 Marks
- **Expression (Grammar, Vocabulary, Coherence)**: 2 Marks

## 💡 Language Tips & Useful Vocabulary
- **Shape & Size**: Spherical, cylindrical, rectangular, miniature, massive, compact.
- **Material & Texture**: Wooden, metallic, plastic, leather, smooth, rough, glossy, fragile.
- **Sentence Starters**:
  - *"One of my most prized possessions is..."*
  - *"It is made of..."*
  - *"The most striking feature of this object is..."*

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Describing a story about the object rather than the object itself.
- Missing out on the material or exact physical dimensions.
- Using plain vocabulary instead of vivid adjectives.

## 🌟 Model Example

**Q: Write a descriptive paragraph in 100-120 words describing your favorite wristwatch.**

**My Favorite Wristwatch**
One of my most prized possessions is the wristwatch gifted to me by my grandfather on my twelfth birthday. It is a classic, analog watch with a sleek, metallic silver body. The dial is perfectly circular, featuring a deep navy-blue background with glowing, radium-coated numbers and hands that make it easy to read in the dark. It is attached to a sturdy, dark brown genuine leather strap that feels smooth against the skin. Aside from telling the time accurately, it has a small date window on the right side. This watch is not just a tool for me; it is a symbol of my grandfather's love and a constant reminder to value time.

## ✍️ Practice Prompts

1. **Describe your favorite toy or gadget** that you use every day. Focus on its appearance, features, and why you love it. (100-120 words)
2. **Write a descriptive paragraph about a lost umbrella**, providing specific details so that whoever finds it can return it to you. (100-120 words)
