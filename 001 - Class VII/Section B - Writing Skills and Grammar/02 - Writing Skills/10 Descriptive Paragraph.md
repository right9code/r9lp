# Descriptive Paragraph (Scene/Event)

## 📌 What is a Descriptive Paragraph about a Scene/Event?
A descriptive paragraph about an **event or scene** captures the action, atmosphere, and energy of a specific moment in time (e.g., a sports day, a festival celebration, an accident, or a rainy afternoon). It aims to transport the reader directly into the middle of the action.

## 📝 Format / Structure (Word Limit: 100-120 words)

1. **Heading/Title**: Relevant name of the event/scene.
2. **Introduction (1-2 sentences)**: 
   - State what the event is, where it is taking place, and when.
3. **Body (Action & Atmosphere) (5-6 sentences)**:
   - **Chronological details**: How did it start? What is happening right now?
   - **Sensory details**: Sights (decorations, crowds), Sounds (cheering, music), Feelings/Smells.
   - **People's reactions**: Describe the mood (joyous, chaotic, tense).
4. **Conclusion (1-2 sentences)**:
   - The ending of the event or the overall lasting impression of the scene.

## ⚖️ CBSE Marking Scheme (Typically 5 Marks)
- **Format (Title)**: 1 Mark
- **Content**: 2 Marks
- **Expression (Grammar, Vocabulary, Coherence)**: 2 Marks

## 💡 Language Tips & Useful Vocabulary
- **Dynamic Verbs**: Cheering, echoing, rushing, glittering, thundering, bustling.
- **Adjectives**: Electrifying, spectacular, chaotic, memorable, gloomy, vibrant.
- **Sequence words**: Initially, suddenly, meanwhile, finally, as soon as.
- **Sentence Starters**:
  - *"The atmosphere was absolutely electric..."*
  - *"Everywhere you looked, people were..."*
  - *"The highlight of the event was..."*

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Making it a boring report instead of a descriptive scene. Use adjectives to paint a picture!
- Jumping back and forth in time. Keep the description moving forward logically.

## 🌟 Model Example

**Q: Write a descriptive paragraph describing the scene of your school’s Annual Sports Day.**

**The Annual Sports Day**
Our school’s Annual Sports Day was a spectacular display of energy and enthusiasm. The school ground was beautifully decorated with colorful flags and banners fluttering in the cool morning breeze. The atmosphere was electrifying, filled with the loud cheering of students and the rhythmic beating of drums from the school band. Athletes, dressed in their bright house uniforms, lined up enthusiastically on the tracks. The highlight of the day was the 100-meter sprint, where the runners dashed like lightning amidst thunderous applause from the spectators. Parents and teachers continuously encouraged the participants. The event concluded with an impressive medal ceremony, leaving everyone exhausted but brimming with immense pride and joy.

## ✍️ Practice Prompts

1. **Describe a rainy day scene** outside your window, focusing on the dark clouds, the sound of rain, and how people are reacting on the streets. (100-120 words)
2. **Write a descriptive paragraph about the scene at a railway station**, highlighting the noise, the rush of passengers, and the arrival of a train. (100-120 words)
