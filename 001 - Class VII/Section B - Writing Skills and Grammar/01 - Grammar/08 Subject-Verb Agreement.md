### A. Complete the conversation by choosing the correct form of the verb.

*   **Nidhi**: This table beside the window **offers** a good view.
*   **Vineet**: Yes, the view from here of the mountains, clouds and sky **is** beautiful, isn't it?
*   **Nidhi**: The music and the lighting **are** both perfect. Here's the menu.
*   **Vineet**: Every item listed here **sounds** so French, but none of them **is** expensive.
*   **Nidhi**: Can you see if the house **serves** Chinese?
