### D. Editing (Incorrect Word | Correct Word)

1. can | **must** (or **should**)
2. could | **should**
3. x | x
4. would | **should**
5. would | **may** (or **will**)
6. used to | **may** (or **can**)
7. x | x
8. might | **can** (or **should**)
9. cannot | **should not** (or **must not**)
