### A. Mark whether the highlighted verbs in these sentences are simple present (SPr), present continuous (PrC), simple past (SP), past continuous (PC), simple future (SF) or future continuous (FC).

1. Why **are you** not **carrying** a bag? — **PrC** (Present Continuous)
2. The cook **will cut** the vegetables. — **SF** (Simple Future)
3. She **finished** the letter. I **posted** it. — **SP** (Simple Past)
4. We **were thinking** of visiting you. — **PC** (Past Continuous)
5. Mira **sold** her paintings at an exhibition hall built last year. — **SP** (Simple Past)
6. When Sita **arrived**, I **was taking** a bath. — **SP** (Simple Past) and **PC** (Past Continuous)
7. The train **will be leaving** in an hour. — **FC** (Future Continuous)
8. He **was studying** in the bedroom when the lights **went** out. — **PC** (Past Continuous) and **SP** (Simple Past)
9. Sam **visits** his uncle every Sunday. — **SPr** (Simple Present)
10. We **shall be trekking** in the area before he **leaves** for London. — **FC** (Future Continuous) and **SPr** (Simple Present)
11. When I **visited** the monument the last time, I **found** it crumbling. — **SP** (Simple Past)
12. My sister **is washing** a lot of clothes as she **will be going** out of town. — **PrC** (Present Continuous) and **FC** (Future Continuous)
13. The children **are playing** outside, while their mothers **are drinking** tea. — **PrC** (Present Continuous)
14. Rahim **was walking** down the road when he **noticed** something strange. — **PC** (Past Continuous) and **SP** (Simple Past)
15. Jiya's father **was driving** the car when the accident **took** place. No one **was** injured. — **PC** (Past Continuous) and **SP** (Simple Past)
