### A. Fill in the blanks with suitable prepositions. (Sample Answers)

1. You must leave **by** (or **before**) sunset.
2. The child hid **behind** the curtains.
3. Our class teacher will return **within** (or **in**) a week.
4. I jumped **into** the lake **with** the others.
5. I shall return **on** the fifth **of** the month.
6. The event proved to be an eye-opener **for** many.
7. I think you should leave **after** completing your work.
8. Keep the biscuits **on** the shelf **above** (or **beside**) the sink.
9. He stood **before** the principal **with** his head bowed.
10. I arrived **in** time **for** the inauguration ceremony.
11. You will find him sitting quietly **in** a chair **by** (or **at**) the fireside.
12. Five years **after** I was born, this house was built **by** my father.
13. He has come **from** London and will be staying **in** this country **for** a month.
14. The American tourists plan to walk **through** a village **in** the hills. They will stay there **for** a week.
