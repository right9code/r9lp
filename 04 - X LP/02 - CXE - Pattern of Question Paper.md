# PATTERN OF QUESTION PAPER 

## WEIGHTAGE SUMMARY:
| **Section** | **Title**                 | **Marks**    |
| :---------- | :------------------------ | :----------- |
| A           | Reading Section           | 20 M         |
| B           | Grammar & Writing Section | 20 M         |
| C           | Literature Section        | 40 M         |
| **Total**   |                           | **80 Marks** |

---
## I. SECTION A: READING SKILLS (20 MARKS)

- ### 1. **Discursive Passage** (10 M)
	- **Nature:** Opinion-based, persuasive, or reflective text.
	- **Length:** 400-450 words.
	- **Question types:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs), Objective Type, and Short Answer questions.
- ### 2. **Case-Based Factual Passage** (10 M)
	- **Nature:** Data-driven text with visual inputs (charts, graphs, or statistical reports).
	* **Length:** 200-250 words.
	- **Question types:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs), Objective Type, and Short Answer questions.
---
## II. SECTION B: GRAMMAR AND WRITING SECTION (20 MARKS)
- ### 3. **Grammar** (10 M)
	- **Options:** 10/12 Questions
	- **Marking:** 10 x 1 M = 10 M
	- **Topics:**
	    * Determiners
	    * Tenses
	    * Modals
	    * Subject-Verb Concord
	    * Reported Speech (Commands, Requests, Statements, Questions)
- ### 4. **Formal Letter** (05 M)
	- **Options:** 1/2 Questions
	- **Word Limit:** 100-120 words
	- **Topics:** 
		- Letter to Editor 
		- Civic Authority Letters
			- Municipality and Govt Agencies 
			- RWA (Resident Welfare Association)
		- Business Letters
			- Purchase/Order Placement 
			- Inquiry 
			- Complaint 
		- Institution Letters 
			- School, College, University etc. 
- ### 5. **Analytical Paragraph** (05 M)
	- **Options:** 1/2 Questions
	- **Word Limit:** 100-120 words
	- **Topics:** Based on a Map, Chart, Graph, or Cues.
---
## III. SECTION C: LITERATURE SECTION (40 MARKS)
- ### 6. **Prose/Drama Extract** (05 M)
	- **Options:** 1/2 Extracts
	- **Source:** FIRST FLIGHT / FOOTPRINTS WITHOUT FEET
	- **Word limit:** 
		- One line for 1 M Descriptive Question
		- 40 words for 2 M Descriptive Question. 
	- **Question types:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs), Objective Type, and Short Answer questions.
- ### 7. **Poetry Extract** (05 M)
	- **Options:** 1/2 Extracts
	- **Source:** FIRST FLIGHT / FOOTPRINTS WITHOUT FEET
	- **Word limit:** 
		- One line for 1 M Descriptive Question
		- 40 words for 2 M Descriptive Question. 
	- **Question types:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs), Objective Type, and Short Answer questions.
- ### 8. **SHORT ANSWER TYPE**  (12 M)
	- **Options:** 4/5 Questions
	- **Marking:** 4 x 3 M = 12 M
	- **Source:** FIRST FLIGHT
	- **Word limit:** 40-50 words per question
- ### 9. **SHORT ANSWER TYPE** (06 M)
	- **Options:** 2/3 Questions
	- **Marking:** 2 x 3 M = 6 M
	- **Source:** FOOTPRINTS WITHOUT FEET
	- **Word limit:** 40-50 words per question
- ### 10. **LONG ANSWER TYPE** (06 M)
	- **Options:** 1/2 Questions
	- **Marking:** 1 x 6 M = 6 M
	- **Source:** FIRST FLIGHT
	- **Word limit:** 100-120 words per question
- ### 11. **LONG ANSWER TYPE** (06 M)
	- **Options:** 1/2 Questions
	- **Marking:** 1 x 6 M = 6 M
	- **Source:** FOOTPRINTS WITHOUT FEET
	- **Word limit:** 100-120 words per question
