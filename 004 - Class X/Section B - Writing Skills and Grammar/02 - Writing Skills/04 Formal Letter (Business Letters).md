# Formal Letter: Business Letters

Business letters are formal letters written for commercial purposes. They are written to business entities (managers, directors, suppliers, store owners) to inquire about services, place orders, register complaints, or ask for replacements.

*(Note: The general format of all formal letters is the same. Refer to the 'Letter to Editor' notes for the universal 10-step layout).*

## I. Details for Exam
*   **Word Limit:** 100–120 words (Body).
*   **Marks:** 5 Marks (Format 1 + Content 2 + Expression 2).
*   **Tone:** Highly professional, polite, concise, and clear. Do not use emotional language, even when complaining.
*   **Categories:** 
    1. Letter of Inquiry
    2. Letter for Placing an Order
    3. Letter of Complaint / Replacement

---

## II. Types of Business Letters & Specific Layouts

### 1. Letter of Inquiry
Written to gather information about a course, tour package, product, or service.

**Body Structure:**
*   **Para 1 (Introduction):** Mention how you got to know about them (e.g., "With reference to your advertisement in The Times of India dated..."). State the purpose of the letter (e.g., "I wish to inquire about...").
*   **Para 2 (Details):** Ask specific questions. You can use bullet points for clarity.
    *   Duration of the course/tour
    *   Fee structure / Total cost
    *   Eligibility / Batch timings
    *   Boarding and lodging details (for tours)
    *   Discounts available
*   **Para 3 (Conclusion):** Request a prompt reply. "Please send me the detailed brochure and prospectus at the earliest so I can make a decision."

### 2. Letter for Placing an Order
Written to purchase goods in bulk (books, sports equipment, furniture).

**Body Structure:**
*   **Para 1 (Introduction):** Refer to a previous quotation or inquiry. "Thank you for the catalogue and quotation sent on [Date]. We are pleased to place an order for the following items."
*   **Para 2 (The Order Table):** Always use a table to specify the order.
    | S.No. | Name of Item / Title | Brand / Author | Quantity |
    | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
    | 1 | Cricket Bats | SG | 10 |
    | 2 | Footballs | Nivia | 15 |
*   **Para 3 (Conclusion & Terms):** Mention delivery expectations, condition of goods, and payment mode. "Please ensure that all items are in good condition and delivered by [Date]. Payment will be made by cheque upon delivery. Please apply the 15% institutional discount."

### 3. Letter of Complaint / Replacement
Written when you receive defective goods, poor service, or incorrect items.

**Body Structure:**
*   **Para 1 (Introduction):** State the details of the purchase. "I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with a [Product Name] purchased from your store on [Date] against cash memo number [Receipt No]."
*   **Para 2 (The Problem):** Describe the exact defect or issue. "On unpacking the product, I discovered that it is defective. The screen flickers constantly and the volume button does not work."
*   **Para 3 (Action Required):** State what you want them to do. "Since the product is under the one-year warranty, I request you to either repair it immediately or replace it with a new, functioning unit at the earliest."

---

## III. Key Phrases to Memorize

**For Inquiry:**
*   *With reference to your advertisement...*
*   *I am interested in pursuing...*
*   *I would be grateful if you could provide me with the following details...*

**For Order Placement:**
*   *We are pleased to place a bulk order for...*
*   *Kindly find the details of the order below:*
*   *We expect the delivery to be made within a week.*

**For Complaint:**
*   *I regret to inform you that the product I purchased is malfunctioning.*
*   *I am returning the defective item with this letter.*
*   *I hope you will look into this matter urgently and replace the item.*

## IV. Sample Practice Question (Order Placement)

**Question:** You are the Sports Secretary of XYZ School. Write a letter to the Manager of M/s Sports World, placing an order for sports equipment for your school.

**Sample Answer:**

XYZ School  
New Delhi

15 March 2026

The Manager  
M/s Sports World  
Daryaganj, New Delhi

**Subject:** <ins>Placement of order for sports equipment.</ins>

Sir,

With reference to our previous correspondence and the catalogue sent by you on 10th March 2026, we are pleased to place a bulk order for sports equipment for our school.

Please supply the following items:

| S.No. | Item Name | Brand | Quantity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | Cricket Bats | SG | 10 |
| 2 | Footballs | Nivia | 15 |
| 3 | Badminton Rackets | Yonex | 20 |
| 4 | Tennis Balls | Cosco | 50 |

Kindly ensure that all items are of the latest manufacturing batch and are in perfect condition. Any defective items will be returned at your cost. We expect the delivery to reach our school premises by 25th March 2026. 

As agreed, please apply the 20% institutional discount on the total bill. Payment will be made via a crossed cheque upon the successful delivery of the goods.

Thanking you

Yours sincerely,
*(Signature)*
Sports Secretary
