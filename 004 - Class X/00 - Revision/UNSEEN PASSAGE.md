### **Reading Passage**

By 2030, Artificial Intelligence (AI) is projected to automate 300 million jobs globally, triggering a 45% reduction in routine, monotonous manual labor. However, this disruption is not purely destructive; it will boost the global GDP by 7%, adding $7 trillion to the economy. Consequently, while simple bookkeeping roles disappear, hiring for jobs requiring emotional intelligence has surged by 25%. Rather than rendering humans obsolete, AI will act as a collaborative co-pilot, guiding the workforce toward advanced cognitive roles.

**(iii)** Emotional intelligence (or soft skills)

**(vii)** destructive
