### Case Study: Solar Energy in District X Schools  

In 2024, District X transitioned 500 government schools to solar power.  

By 2026, the project reduced electricity grid dependency by 75% and cut carbon emissions by 1,200 metric tons.  

Schools saved an average of ₹45,000 monthly per power bill, allowing authorities to redirect ₹2.7 crores into upgrading science labs.  

However, the report noted two issues: a 15% drop in solar efficiency during monsoon months due to cloud cover, and a lack of local technical staff, which delayed repairs by an average of 12 days per breakdown.
