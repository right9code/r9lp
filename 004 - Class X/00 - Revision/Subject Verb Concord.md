# Subject-Verb Concord: Test Questions (Fill in the Blanks)

## Instructions
Fill in the blanks with the correct form of the verb provided in the brackets.

1. The quality of these mangoes ________ (is/are) not very good.
2. Neither the teacher nor the students ________ (has/have) the keys to the lab.
3. Gulliver’s Travels ________ (was/were) written by Jonathan Swift.
4. Bread and butter ________ (is/are) a wholesome breakfast for many people.
5. A pair of binoculars ________ (was/were) found near the trekking trail.
6. The committee ________ (is/are) divided in their opinions regarding the new policy.
7. Economics ________ (is/are) a subject that requires logical reasoning.
8. Every man and every woman ________ (was/were) given a floral welcome.
9. Two thousand rupees ________ (is/are) too much to pay for this shirt.
10. The leader, along with his followers, ________ (has/have) arrived at the venue.
