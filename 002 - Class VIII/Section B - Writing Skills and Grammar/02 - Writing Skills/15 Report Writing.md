## 🌟 Model Example 2: Accident Report

**Q: You are Aman/Amita, a staff reporter for 'The Daily News'. You witnessed a major train derailment near Kanpur station. Write a report covering the incident in 120-150 words.**

**Major Train Derailment Near Kanpur**  
*By Aman, Staff Reporter*

**Kanpur, 15 October:** A major tragedy was averted yesterday when three coaches of the Delhi-bound Shatabdi Express derailed near the Kanpur Central Station at approximately 6:30 AM. Fortunately, no casualties have been reported, though around twenty passengers suffered minor injuries.

According to railway officials, the derailment occurred due to a massive crack in the tracks, likely caused by severe overnight temperature drops. The train was moving at a slow speed of 30 km/hr as it was approaching the station, which prevented the coaches from overturning. Panic gripped the passengers as a loud screeching noise was heard, followed by violent jolts. 

Rescue operations commenced immediately. Local police and railway medical teams reached the spot within fifteen minutes. The injured passengers were given first-aid and shifted to the district hospital. The Railway Minister has ordered a high-level inquiry into the incident to determine any negligence regarding track maintenance. Normal train traffic on the route was restored by late evening.
