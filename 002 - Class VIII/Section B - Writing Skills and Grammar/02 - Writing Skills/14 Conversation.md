## 🌟 Model Example 2: Formal Interaction

**Q: Write a conversation between a student and the school librarian regarding the procedure to issue library books and the penalty for late returns.**

*(Inside the school library)*

**Student**: Good morning, sir.  
**Librarian**: Good morning. How can I help you today?  
**Student**: Sir, I am a new student in Class VIII-B. I would like to know the procedure for issuing books from the library.  
**Librarian**: Welcome to the school! First, you need to get your library card made. Have you submitted your passport-sized photograph to your class teacher?  
**Student**: Yes, sir. I submitted it yesterday. When will I receive the card?  
**Librarian**: You should get it by tomorrow. Once you have your card, you can browse the shelves and bring the book you want to this desk. We will scan your card and the book's barcode.  
**Student**: How many books can I issue at one time?  
**Librarian**: You can issue a maximum of two books at a time.  
**Student**: And for how long can I keep them?  
**Librarian**: The standard issue period is 14 days. You must return or renew the books within that time.  
**Student**: What happens if I return a book late?  
**Librarian**: There is a late fee penalty of ₹5 per day. Also, if a book is lost or damaged, you will have to pay the full replacement cost.  
**Student**: Understood, sir. Thank you so much for the information.  
**Librarian**: You're welcome. Happy reading!
