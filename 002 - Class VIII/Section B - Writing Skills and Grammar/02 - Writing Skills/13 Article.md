## 🌟 Model Example 2: Social Awareness

**Q: You are Ramesh/Reena. Write an article for your school magazine on the topic: "The Importance of Promoting Local Artisans and Handicrafts." (120-150 words)**

**Reviving Our Roots: Promoting Local Artisans**  
*By Reena*

India is a land of rich cultural heritage, widely known across the globe for its exquisite handicrafts and traditional arts. From the intricate Pashmina shawls of Kashmir to the vibrant terracotta pottery of Bengal, our local artisans weave magic with their hands. However, in today’s era of rapid industrialization and mass-produced factory goods, these traditional artisans are struggling for survival.

The primary cause of their struggle is the lack of exposure and the inability to compete with the incredibly low prices of machine-made products. Consequently, many skilled craftsmen are abandoning their ancestral professions to work as daily wage laborers in cities. If this trend continues, we risk losing a vital part of our cultural identity forever.

To prevent this, it is our collective responsibility to support local artisans. We must consciously choose to buy indigenous products during festivals and for gifting. The government should also provide better platforms, such as online portals and frequent handicraft fairs, to help them reach a global audience. By promoting local handicrafts, we not only preserve our cultural heritage but also boost the rural economy.
