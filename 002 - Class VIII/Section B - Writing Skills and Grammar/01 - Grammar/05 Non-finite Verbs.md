### D. Identify the participles and their types.

1. **tiring**: Present participle (used as subjective complement)
2. **Learned**: Participial adjective
3. **weeping**: Present participle
4. **selected**: Past participle (Participial adjective)
5. **Beaten**: Past participle
6. **Drawing**: Present participle
7. **Having finished**: Perfect participle
8. **delightful**: (Adjective - not a participle); **echoed**: (Finite verb) -> *Note: If 'delightful' was intended as 'delighting', it would be a participle.*
