## Perfect and Perfect Continuous Tenses: Exercise Solutions

Below are the complete exercises and solutions from **11 Perfect Tenses.pdf** based on the grammar rules for present, past, and future perfect and continuous forms.

### G. Fill in the blanks with the correct form (Present Perfect or Past Perfect) of the verbs given in brackets.

1. **Q:** We ________ (know) each other for many years.
   **A:** We **have known** each other for many years.
2. **Q:** He ________ (work) in this office for many years before he was transferred.
   **A:** He **had worked** in this office for many years before he was transferred.
3. **Q:** I ________ (finish) my homework. Now I can play.
   **A:** I **have finished** my homework. Now I can play.
4. **Q:** By the time the police arrived, the dacoits ________ (flee).
   **A:** By the time the police arrived, the dacoits **had fled**.
5. **Q:** I ________ (not see) him since we left school.
   **A:** I **have not seen** him since we left school.
6. **Q:** The patient ________ (die) before the doctor arrived.
   **A:** The patient **had died** before the doctor arrived.
7. **Q:** I ________ (write) the letter. Please post it.
   **A:** I **have written** the letter. Please post it.
8. **Q:** I ________ (read) the book you gave me.
   **A:** I **have read** the book you gave me.
9. **Q:** The rain ________ (stop) when we stepped out of the house.
   **A:** The rain **had stopped** when we stepped out of the house.
10. **Q:** We ________ (reach) the station before the train arrived.
    **A:** We **had reached** the station before the train arrived.
11. **Q:** My sister ________ (buy) a new car recently.
    **A:** My sister **has bought** a new car recently.
12. **Q:** I ________ (not see) the movie yet.
    **A:** I **have not seen** the movie yet.
13. **Q:** The bus ________ (leave) already.
    **A:** The bus **has left** already.
14. **Q:** The match ________ (start) before we reached the stadium.
    **A:** The match **had started** before we reached the stadium.
15. **Q:** ________ you ________ (do) your homework now?
    **A:** **Have** you **done** your homework now?
16. **Q:** ________ you ever ________ (travel) in a hovercraft?
    **A:** **Have** you ever **traveled** in a hovercraft?
17. **Q:** By the time I reached the auditorium, the concert ________ (start).
    **A:** By the time I reached the auditorium, the concert **had started**.
18. **Q:** How many sums ________ you ________ (solve) so far?
    **A:** How many sums **have** you **solved** so far?
19. **Q:** The children came to the birthday party at 4 o'clock yesterday, but before that Shena and Taanish ________ (decorate) the room.
    **A:** The children came to the birthday party at 4 o'clock yesterday, but before that Shena and Taanish **had decorated** the room.
20. **Q:** Uday asked Manish if he ________ (read) Matilda?
    **A:** Uday asked Manish if he **had read** Matilda?

### H. Fill in the blanks with the perfect continuous tense of the verb given in the brackets.

1. **Q:** The soup ________ (simmer) for quite some time now. Why don't you switch off the gas?
   **A:** The soup **has been simmering** for quite some time now.
2. **Q:** We ________ (wait) for a bus for an hour when a car stopped by and offered us a lift.
   **A:** We **had been waiting** for a bus for an hour when a car stopped by and offered us a lift.
3. **Q:** Until last year, our school team ________ (win) the Inter School Football Cup.
   **A:** Until last year, our school team **had been winning** the Inter School Football Cup.
4. **Q:** Someone ________ (try) to pick this lock!
   **A:** Someone **has been trying** to pick this lock!
5. **Q:** He ________ (ring) the bell for five minutes. But no one opened the door.
   **A:** He **had been ringing** the bell for five minutes.
6. **Q:** Ms Jaya ________ (act) in films for twenty years when she decided to join politics.
   **A:** Ms Jaya **had been acting** in films for twenty years when she decided to join politics.
7. **Q:** The minister ________ (visit) these parts recently. This is quite common before the elections.
   **A:** The minister **has been visiting** these parts recently.
8. **Q:** The candidates ________ (wait) for the interview since ten o'clock. The committee has not called any of them yet.
   **A:** The candidates **have been waiting** for the interview since ten o'clock.
9. **Q:** He ________ (work) on this problem for three hours now.
   **A:** He **has been working** on this problem for three hours now.
10. **Q:** For the last six months, he ________ (take) Ayurvedic medicines for his asthma.
    **A:** For the last six months, he **has been taking** Ayurvedic medicines for his asthma.
