### C. Rewrite with Adjective Forms

1.  **Original:** I was surprised by his foolishness.  
    **Rewrite:** His **foolish** behaviour surprised me.
2.  **Original:** He behaved like a child.  
    **Rewrite:** He behaved in a **childish** manner.
3.  **Original:** He spoke with bluntness.  
    **Rewrite:** He was **blunt** in his speech.
4.  **Original:** London is a city with a large population.  
    **Rewrite:** London is a **populous** city.
5.  **Original:** You must deal with the situation boldly.  
    **Rewrite:** You must be **bold** in dealing with the situation.
6.  **Original:** You should maintain silence in the library.  
    **Rewrite:** You should be **silent** in the library.
7.  **Original:** You have the freedom to do what you wish.  
    **Rewrite:** You are **free** to do what you wish.
8.  **Original:** The children are playing happily in the park.  
    **Rewrite:** The **happy** children are playing in the park.
9.  **Original:** We hoped that the deal would be clinched soon.  
    **Rewrite:** We were **hopeful** that the deal would be clinched soon.
10. **Original:** I was impressed by the wildness of the surroundings.  
    **Rewrite:** I was impressed by the **wild** surroundings.
11. **Original:** I was impressed by Jayant's determination to succeed.  
    **Rewrite:** I was impressed by Jayant's **determined** effort to succeed.
12. **Original:** The teenagers bravely decided to solve the mystery.  
    **Rewrite:** The **brave** teenagers decided to solve the mystery.
