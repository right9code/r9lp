### A. Correct the errors in these sentences, if any

1.  **Original:** Every one of them were punished.
    **Corrected:** Every one of them **was** punished.
2.  **Original:** Much of the truth were not disclosed.
    **Corrected:** Much of the truth **was** not disclosed.
3.  **Original:** Neither Gita nor Hira were present today.
    **Corrected:** Neither Gita nor Hira **was** present today.
4.  **Original:** Diamonds, as well as gold, are found in the area.
    **Corrected:** **No error.** (The verb agrees with the first subject, "Diamonds," which is plural).
5.  **Original:** The news of the series of accidents are true.
    **Corrected:** The news of the series of accidents **is** true.
6.  **Original:** One of the girls in the team were sent back.
    **Corrected:** One of the girls in the team **was** sent back.
7.  **Original:** Two lakh rupees are a lot of money for me.
    **Corrected:** Two lakh rupees **is** a lot of money for me.
8.  **Original:** The boys or their teacher have left this behind.
    **Corrected:** The boys or their teacher **has** left this behind.
9.  **Original:** Many of the children has passed the written test.
    **Corrected:** Many of the children **have** passed the written test.
10. **Original:** Bread and butter were part of his daily breakfast.
    **Corrected:** Bread and butter **was** part of his daily breakfast.
11. **Original:** She is one of those persons who knows the matter.
    **Corrected:** She is one of those persons who **know** the matter.
12. **Original:** The students, as well as the teacher, are here.
    **Corrected:** **No error.**
13. **Original:** The orator and statesman have arrived for the inauguration.
    **Corrected:** The orator and statesman **has** arrived for the inauguration.
14. **Original:** A large number of people is waiting to meet the national leader.
    **Corrected:** A large number of people **are** waiting to meet the national leader.
15. **Original:** The scholar and the poet is here for the prize distribution ceremony.
    **Corrected:** The scholar and the poet **are** here for the prize distribution ceremony.
