# Informal Invitation Letter

## 📌 What is an Informal Invitation Letter?
An **informal invitation letter** is written to invite friends, relatives, or close acquaintances to personal events such as a birthday party, an anniversary, a housewarming, or a casual get-together. The tone is warm, personal, and friendly, completely avoiding official jargon.

## 📝 Format / Structure (Word Limit: 50-100 words)

The format is similar to a regular informal letter but is usually shorter and more direct.

1. **Sender’s Address**: Top left corner.
2. **Date**: Below the address (e.g., *24 May 2026*).
3. **Salutation**: *Dear [First Name],*
4. **Body of the Letter**:
   - **The Invite**: Clearly state what the occasion is and explicitly invite the person.
   - **The Details**: Mention the **Date**, **Time**, and **Venue** of the event clearly.
   - **Personal Touch**: Add a line about how much their presence would mean to you or what fun activities are planned.
5. **Subscription (Closing)**: *Yours lovingly,* / *Yours affectionately,*
6. **Sender’s Name**: First name only.

## ⚖️ CBSE Marking Scheme (Typically 3-4 Marks)
- **Format**: 1 Mark
- **Content (Occasion, Date, Time, Venue)**: 1-2 Marks
- **Expression (Friendly tone, grammar)**: 1 Mark

## 💡 Language Tips & Useful Phrases
- **Opening phrases**:
  - *"I am thrilled to invite you to..."*
  - *"You will be glad to know that..."*
  - *"It gives me immense pleasure to invite you and your family to..."*
- **Adding warmth**:
  - *"The party won't be the same without you."*
  - *"Looking forward to celebrating together."*

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid
- **Forgetting Event Details**: An invitation is useless if you forget to mention *when* and *where* it is happening.
- **Using formal language**: Do not use "Respected" or "Yours faithfully."
- **Writing too much**: Unlike a regular informal letter where you discuss life and health at length, an invitation should get straight to the point.

## 🌟 Model Example

**Q: You are Ankit/Ankita. You are organizing a surprise 50th birthday party for your father. Write an informal letter inviting your uncle, who lives in Chandigarh, to the party.**

14, Green Park  
New Delhi  

15 October 2026  

Dear Uncle Suresh,  

I hope you and Aunt are doing well. You will be glad to know that dad is turning 50 next week. To celebrate this milestone, mom and I are organizing a surprise birthday party for him. 

It gives me immense pleasure to invite you and Aunt to the celebration. The party will be held at our residence on Sunday, 25th October 2026, starting at 7:00 PM. We have arranged for dad's favorite live music band and a wonderful dinner. 

Since it is a surprise, please do not mention it to dad when you speak to him! The celebration will be incomplete without your presence. We are eagerly waiting to see you.

Yours lovingly,  
Ankit

## ✍️ Practice Prompts

1. Write an informal letter to your best friend inviting them to your house for a weekend pajama party to celebrate the end of your final exams. Mention the date and activities planned.
2. Your family has just moved into a new apartment. Write an informal letter to your cousin inviting them to the housewarming ceremony.
